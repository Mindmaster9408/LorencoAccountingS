Period Management

Create and lock accounting periods to prevent backdated journal entries

How it works: Locking a period blocks all write operations (journals, invoices, bank allocations) dated within that period. Existing posted journals and reports are unaffected — lock is read-only enforcement, not deletion. Unlocking a period requires admin access and is audit-logged.
