AI Settings

⚠️ Administrator Access Required
Only company administrators can modify AI settings. All AI features are disabled by default.
ℹ️ AI Add-On "Sean"
The AI assistant is optional and can be enabled/disabled at company, capability, and user levels. All AI actions are audited and can be reversed. AI must pass the same validations as human users.

Company-Level AI Control

AI Capabilities

Configure individual AI capabilities with specific modes and confidence thresholds.

Capability Enabled Mode Min Confidence Description
Bank Allocation Auto-allocate bank transactions to accounts
Bank Reconciliation Auto-reconcile bank statements
Journal Preparation Generate journal entries from documents
Report Preparation Generate financial reports and analysis

Mode Descriptions:

  • OFF: AI capability is disabled
  • SUGGEST: AI returns suggestions only (no drafts created)
  • DRAFT: AI creates drafts for human review and approval
  • AUTO: AI executes directly (requires admin/accountant role + confidence threshold)
